Labradors

Originally the Labrador, called St.Johns water dogs at the time, were bred in Canada. In Canada, they helped fishermen by grabbing fish that fell through the nets, haul nets, and also retrieved the fishermans hats that blew away! Later they were brought to England where they learned to retrieve the ducks that were taken down by the hunters. Today they are still amazing hunting dogs! How easily Labs can be trained has been recongnized and set to work being: Service dogs, Guide dogs, Search and rescue dogs,Therapy dogs, and more!

Here are some dog types

1. BEAGADOR (Beagle + Labrador) 2. LABRABULL (Labrador + American Pitbull Terrier) 3. BORADOR (Labrador + Border Collie)
4. AUSSIEDOR (Australian Shepherd + Labrador) 5. LABMARANER (Labrador + Weimaraner) 6. SHEPRADOR(Labrador + German Shepard)
