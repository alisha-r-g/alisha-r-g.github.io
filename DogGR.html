Golden Retrievers

The Golden Retriever was bred to collect meat from bushes, cliffs, and small ponds and streams, from far away. Golden retrievers were good at this because of their soft mouths so they can deliver meat to the hunter in one piece. But as to today, there are fewer hunters. Which means that most Golden Retrievers aren't technically still used for hunting. Then people realized that Golden Retrievers are amazing family pets! and that's the story of the Golden Retiever!
