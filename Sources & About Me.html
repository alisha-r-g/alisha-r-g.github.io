About the Creator

Hello, I’m Alisha!

I have always been passionate about animals, with a special fondness for elephants and dogs. This deep love for our furry friends inspired me to create the "Dogtionary" — a dedicated resource aimed at celebrating and educating others about the diverse and wonderful world of dog breeds.

My journey into the realm of dog breeds began with my Mini Bernedoodle puppy, Claudia, who has stolen my heart with her playful spirit and endearing personality. As Claudia and I explore the many facets of dog breeds together, I am excited to share what I’ve learned with you.

Currently, the "Dogtionary" features detailed profiles on three main dog breeds. However, my mission doesn’t stop there. I am continuously expanding the content to include even more breeds, providing you with comprehensive and up-to-date information.

I hope that through the "Dogtionary," you gain a deeper appreciation for the incredible variety of dog breeds and their unique characteristics. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing this journey with you!

Warm regards,

Alisha
